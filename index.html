Kitties good for your health - owning a cat has been shown to reduce stress levels, improve cardiovascular health, and promote relaxation. They are fun to watch, love, cuddle and spend time with. Cats have soft fur that feels good to pet and stroke, and they will reward you with a purr. If you're looking for a pet that can help you relax, and put the sparkle back into your eye, a cat may be perfect for you. They're simply adorable and good for your health!

Cats have their own unique personalities, from frisky and playful to stubborn and lazy. There are cats for everyone’s personal needs. Kids love to play with kittens and watch them tumble around with each other. Then there are those older folks who prefer a gentler feline friend that will sit on their laps and simply soak up the love. No matter what personality you have there is a cat that will make a great companion for you!

Cats are known for being intelligent creatures that can learn tricks and behaviors just like dogs. Cats are curious and inquisitive so they're always exploring and getting into unforeseen mischief!
